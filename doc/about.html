Fettuccine

A Webcam Aggregator App

Version 0.9.5

Fettuccine is an app designed to collect your favorite webcams and display them in a convenient and efficient manner. For more information, please visit the Fettuccine website.

This is a free and open-source program. However, if you have enjoyed it, please consider donating $1 (or whatever amount you feel appropriate). These funds will help me to continue working on this and other apps. Thank you!
